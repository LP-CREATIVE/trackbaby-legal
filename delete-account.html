Totling — Delete Your Account

Published by LP Creative Studio · Last updated June 5, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Totling account and the data associated with it.

How to request deletion

Send an email to sebastian.phillips44@gmail.com from the email address you use to sign in to Totling, with the subject line:

Delete my account

We use your sending address to verify the request. No other information is required.

What gets deleted

When you request deletion, we permanently remove the data tied to your account from our cloud database, including:

  • Your account and email address
  • Your caregiver display name
  • The baby profiles in your household (name, birthdate, measurements, any profile photo)
  • All tracking events you and your household logged (feeds, sleep, diapers, pumping, growth, notes, etc.)
  • Your schedule items and household/invite records

Data stored locally on your device is removed when you uninstall the app.

Timeline

We complete deletion requests within 30 days of receiving them. We do not retain a backup copy of your data beyond what is needed to complete the deletion.

Note on shared households

Totling data can be shared within a household (you and other caregivers you invite). If you only want to stop sharing without deleting everything, you can leave the household from within the app instead. A full account-deletion request removes your account and the cloud data for your household as described above.

Questions

Email sebastian.phillips44@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
