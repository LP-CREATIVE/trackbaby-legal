TrackBaby — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 4, 2026

TrackBaby (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a baby-tracking app published by LP Creative Studio. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We keep it short and plain because the app collects very little.

Contact: sebastian.phillips44@gmail.com

Who this app is for

TrackBaby is designed for parents and caregivers (adults) to record their own baby’s daily activities. It is not directed to children and is not intended to be used by children. The information you enter about a baby (name, birthdate, activities) is entered by you, the adult account holder, and is treated as your personal data.

We only collect what’s needed to run the app and sync it between caregivers:

We do not collect: precise or approximate location, contacts, advertising IDs, browsing history, or audio. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not show ads.

How your data is stored and synced

  • Data you enter is stored locally on your device first (the app works offline).
  • If you sign in, your data is synced to our cloud database so it is backed up and shared with members of your household. Cloud hosting and authentication are provided by Supabase (our data processor). Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

How your data is shared

  • With your household: When you create or join a family/household and invite others, those members can see the baby profile, events, and schedule for that household. That is the intended function.
  • With service providers: Supabase, which hosts our database and handles authentication on our behalf.
  • We never sell your data and never share it with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We may disclose information if required by law.

Permissions the app may request

  • Photos / Camera — only when you choose to attach or take a photo. You can decline; the rest of the app works without it.
  • Notifications — to send schedule reminders, if you enable them.

Data retention and deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In the app: leave a household to stop sharing, or
  • By email: write to sebastian.phillips44@gmail.com from your account email with the subject “Delete my account” and we will delete your account and associated cloud data within 30 days.

When you delete the app, locally stored data on that device is removed by the operating system. Cloud data is removed when you request account deletion as above.

Children’s privacy

TrackBaby is intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly create accounts for anyone under 18. The baby information stored in the app is entered and controlled by the adult account holder.

Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted transport, authenticated access, row-level security so each household only sees its own data). No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we’ll update the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
